About Sully's

Sully’s House is a perfect venue to celebrate any event. We have multiple spaces available: 2 bars, lounge, private outdoor patio, and a beautiful rooftop deck. We can accommodate parties of 10 – 150 guests. 30 HD TV’s, customized and zoned sound system in every room and Audio-Video options. Room rents and spending minimums may apply. 1 block south of the Clybourn Red Line at Halsted and North.
